About Tornekar General Stores

Welcome to *Tornekar General Stores*, your trusted local destination for all your general needs. We are a family-owned and operated business, dedicated to serving our community with quality products and exceptional service.

Our store is proudly managed by the *Tornekar family. The owners are **Mr. Balaji Tornekar* and *Mrs. Savitri Tornekar, who have built this establishment with dedication and a commitment to their customers. They are ably supported by their sons, **Pritam Tornekar* and *Vaibhav Tornekar*, who contribute to the daily operations and ongoing success of the store.

At Tornekar General Stores, we believe in fostering strong community ties and providing a friendly, reliable shopping experience. We look forward to serving you!
